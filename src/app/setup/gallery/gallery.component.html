Our Drinks category is divided into three sub categories namely Hot, Cold and Alcoholic Beverages. We pay special attention to the hot drinks category as it is a very high margin and high volume category with various differentiation possibilities. Cold and Alcoholic drinks are the same everywhere you go and the competition is stiff. With Hot drinks, the source of coffee and tea, the machine used to brew the drinks and the technique and recipe used by the barista all make difference on the loyalty of the customers, hence the bottom line.
