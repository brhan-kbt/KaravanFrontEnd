Welcome to Karavan Coffee

Our Objective!

Karavan Coffee makes its best effort to create a unique place where customers can socialize with each other in a comfortable and relaxing environment while enjoying hot and cold drinks as well as uniquely prepared pastry and food choices.

Our Coffee

~ Karavan Promise ~

Coffee loses its aroma shortly after it is grind. We never buy grind or powdered coffee. Our coffee is tailor-roasted, according to Karavan Coffee secret blend, and grind in-house every morning before brewing.

Our Burger

~ Karavan Promise ~

Our burgers are hand-made every day in the premises. We buy a combination of five different cuts of beef and grind it in-house according to our special recipe. Freezing burgers creates structural damage to the meat, which leads to loss of nutritional value and flavourful juices once the burger is cooked.

Our Ginger Tea

~ Karavan Promise ~

Ginger is highly regarded for its stimulant and medicinal values. We squeeze our ginger in-house. We never use powdered ginger. Try our ginger tea.

Our Cake

~ Karavan Promise ~

We use superior ingredients on all our cakes, which we import ourselves from Europe. KERRY, our supplier from the United Kingdom is a leader in food and beverage ingredients market. Ask for our 2016 'Cake for all Occasions' catalogue of our creative designs and unique styles for all occasions including wedding, birthday, graduation and more.

Our Ginger Tea

~ Karavan Promise ~

The flavour of a Habesha dish is as good as the Berbere it is made from. Our Berbere is home made with 14 secret ingredients and unlike commercial blend, the chilli seed is fully excluded from the final mix for colour, taste and avoid unnecessary heat and discomfort to the stomach.

Our Cake

~ Karavan Promise ~

Bulk squeezed fruit doesn't allow for sugar-free option. It also goes off quickly due to high water content added during bulk squeezing. Our Juice is freshly squeezed per order with sugar-free option.
