Establishment

Our History!

Karavan's history dates back to 1987 E.C, the historic opening of one of the oldest pastry shops in Ethiopia named Bethlehem Pastry at Megenagna. Bethlehem Pastry, known for its vibrant location and affordable pastries, was rebranded in 2004 E.C and all its management and knowledge transferred to Karavan Coffee in 2004 E.C. Karavan quickly expanded into a food and pastry outlet with multiple branches and over 200 product varieties. In 2013 E.C., the company changed its legal status to a Limited Company and established Karavan Trading PLC. Following the establishment, the management of the new trading company are expanding the business into manufacturing, catering and a cattle farming operations that are designed to supply key ingredients to the core business. Currently, Karavan mainly produces and retails cake, coffee, other hot and cold beverages and food items such as Ethiopian traditional food, burgers, sandwiches and pizzas in its five locations at Megenagna, Bole, Semit, Tulu Dimtu and Ayer Tena

Our Philosophy

Layers of happiness!

We believe we are in the business of selling happiness in the form of food and drink. We source superior ingredients and craft beautifully layered products with the ultimate objective of making our customers happy. Customers order our Special Opera Cake because of indulgence, not hunger. As such, most food and beverage items we produce have layer upon layer of flavour, texture and finesse. Our cakes, for example are sliced in three or four layers and filled with various delicious fruits, fillings and syrups before being glazed. Our burgers, pizzas, sandwiches or even macchiato or juice, all come in layers of ingredients aiming to give the ultimate presentation, texture and taste. We believe that each layer of our carefully crafted and decorated product is ultimate source of happiness to our valued customers.
